About Me

A Journey of Faith, Service & Learning

Your Photo Here

Hello! I'm Vivian Antwi, known to many as Sister Vivian. I'm currently pursuing my BSc in Nursing at KAAF University College in Kasoa, where I'm dedicated to building a career in healthcare and making a meaningful impact in people's lives.

My journey has been shaped by a commitment to both education and service. As a devoted member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, I had the privilege of serving a mission after completing my secondary education, an experience that deepened my faith and taught me invaluable lessons about compassion, dedication, and service to others.

Throughout my academic journey, I've not only focused on excellence in my studies but also on bringing joy and creativity to my communities. As the Entertainment Prefect at Anlo Awomefia Senior High School, I discovered my passion for bringing people together and creating memorable experiences.

I believe in the power of continuous learning, dedicated service, and the importance of uplifting those around us. Welcome to my portfolio ‚Äì a glimpse into my journey, my passions, and my aspirations.
